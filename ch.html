教育经历

I am an associate professor in School of Data Science at Fudan University. Before joining Fudan, I was a postdoctoral researcher in Human Language Technology Research Institute at UT Dallas. I got my Ph.D. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2014, supervised by Prof. Kam-fai Wong. I received both of my B.E. and M.E. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2008 and 2010 respectively.
