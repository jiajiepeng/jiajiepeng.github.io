Biography

I am an associate professor in School of Computer Science and Technology at Northwestern Polytechnical University. I got my Ph.D., M.E. and B.E. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2016, 2010 and 2008 respectively, supervised by Prof. Yadong wang. I was a visiting student in Michigan State University from 2010 to 2012, supervised by Prof. Jin Chen. My research focuses on the development of data mining and artificial intelligence algorithms to solve problems in medical and biological informatics.
