Dr. Jiajie Peng (彭佳杰)

I am an associate professor in the School of Computer Science at Northwestern Polytechnical University. I got my Ph.D., M.E. and B.E. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2016, 2010 and 2008 respectively, supervised by Prof. Yadong wang. I was a Research Fellow in the Brigham and Women's Hospital at Harvard Medical School from 2018 to 2020, mentored by Prof. Clemens Scherzer. I was a visiting student at Michigan State University from 2010 to 2012, supervised by Prof. Jin Chen. My research focuses on the development of data mining and artificial intelligence algorithms to solve problems in medicine and biology.
